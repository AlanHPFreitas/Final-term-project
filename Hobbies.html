These are some of my main hobbies in my free time:

Soccer

Football, or soccer in some countries, is a sport that pits two teams, composed of eleven players each (ten field players and a goalkeeper), against each other. The objective is to get the ball into the goal of the opposing team, respecting a series of rules.

Cycling

Cycling is an activity that involves the repetition of a movement and uses the bicycle as a means of locomotion. Cycling can be competitive, recreational, and also practiced as a form of physical activity, both outdoors and indoors.
